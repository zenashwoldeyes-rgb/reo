We want you happy with REO. This policy covers paid annual subscriptions.

## 14-day money-back guarantee
If you're not satisfied, request a **full refund within 14 days** of your initial
purchase, for any reason. Email **licenses@chatapi.run** from your purchase email
with your order details; refunds are issued to the original payment method,
typically within 5–10 business days.

## After 14 days
Annual subscriptions are non-refundable after the first 14 days, including for the
unused portion of a term. You can cancel anytime to stop the next renewal (see
below) — cancelling stops future charges but doesn't refund the current term.

## Renewals
We email a reminder before each annual renewal. To avoid a renewal charge, cancel
**before** the renewal date by emailing **licenses@chatapi.run**. Renewal charges
are subject to the same 14-day window from the renewal date.

## The Free tier
The Free tier is free — nothing to refund.

## Statutory rights
This policy is in addition to any refund or withdrawal rights you have under
applicable consumer-protection law, which it does not limit.

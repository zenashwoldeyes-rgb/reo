**[Company Legal Name] ("REO", "we")** builds privacy-first, local-first security
software. This policy explains the little data we touch — and the lot we don't.

## The short version
REO runs **on your machine**. By default it is air-gapped: it opens no network
sockets, sends no telemetry, no analytics, and no crash reports. Your files,
scan results, and the contents of your device **never leave your machine** and
are **never sent to us**.

## What we DO process (and only this)
| Data | Why | Processor |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Your email + payment details | To sell you a license and email your license token | **Stripe** (payments), **Resend** (the license email) |
| Your license token | Issued at purchase, delivered by email, verified locally | Generated by our webhook on **Cloudflare**; stored only on **your** machine |
| Download/IP logs at install time | Standard server logs when you download the binary | **GitHub Releases** |

We do **not** collect: your files or their contents, scan/detection results,
the names of your processes or network connections, usage analytics, or any
device telemetry. We have no account system and no central database of your
activity.

## The only times REO uses the network
1. **Install** — downloading the binary (one time).
2. **`reo upgrade`** — opening a Stripe checkout link to pay.
3. **`reo activate`** — purely local; it verifies a signed token offline.
4. **`reo infra --apply`** — runs Terraform **on your machine** with **your**
   cloud credentials; those credentials never reach us.
5. **A session you explicitly start with `--cloud`** — REO tells you exactly what
   would be transmitted before anything is sent.

## Sub-processors
Stripe (payments), Resend (transactional email), Cloudflare (the license-issuing
webhook), GitHub (binary distribution). We share only what each needs for its
function above.

## Your rights
Because we hold almost nothing about you (essentially: your purchase email), data
requests are simple. To access or delete what we have (your purchase record /
email), contact **licenses@chatapi.run**. We honor applicable GDPR/CCPA rights.
